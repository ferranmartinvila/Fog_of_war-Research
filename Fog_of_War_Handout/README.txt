FOG OF WAR HANDOUT

This tutorial of how to make a basic fog of war for a RTS videogame
is focused to people that knows the basic concepts of c/c++ programming. 

If you don't get the correct result and don't know how to solve it
check the Solutions folder. 
There you can find screenshoots of the TODOs code solved.


Content:

Player entity spawn at cell [1,1],
there’s an ally entity at cell [25,5],
a neutral entity at cell [20,20],
and an enemy entity at cell [5,15].


Controls:

WASD: To move our entity around the map.
F1: Activate/deactivate debug mode.
